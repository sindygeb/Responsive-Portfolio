- About -

This mermaid is an illustration created for a work project in Fall 2018.


Hailing from Minneapolis, Minnesota (brr...), I studied graphic design and earned a bachelor's degree from the University of Minnesota.


After freelancing for a few years, I went back to school to expand my skill set and earned an associate's degree learning about video game art and animation.


Spending the last few years as a UX designer, animator, illustrator and graphic artist, I just couldn't shake this whole learning thing, and enrolled in a full stack coding bootcamp. I'm hoping to work on a multitude of fun projects, that incorporate my design saavy along with my technical know how.


In my spare time, I love playing games - video, board, card - you name it, spending time with my two crazy dogs, fellow design-nerd-and-geek husband, and my beautiful little daughter/inspiration.
